Seven Day Wild Life Tour

7 Days | 6 Nights

Tour Plans

  • Day 1 Arrival in Colombo and Travel to Pinnawala
    • Morning: Arrive in Colombo and transfer to Pinnawala.
    • Afternoon: Visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. Spend time observing and learning about the care of orphaned elephants and enjoy feeding and bathing sessions.
    • Evening: Check into a nearby hotel and relax.
  • Day 2 Pinnawala to Sigiriya
    • Morning: Travel to Sigiriya (about 2-3 hours by road).
    • Afternoon: Explore Sigiriya Rock Fortress. The surrounding area is home to various bird species and offers panoramic views of the forest.
    • Evening: Enjoy a local dinner and prepare for the next day’s wildlife adventure.
  • Day 3 Sigiriya to Minneriya National Park
    • Morning: Head to Minneriya National Park for a safari (approximately 1-hour drive). Minneriya is renowned for its large elephant gatherings, particularly during the dry season.
    • Afternoon: Enjoy a picnic lunch inside the park and continue your safari.
    • Evening: Return to Sigiriya for the night.
  • Day 4 Sigiriya to Kandy
    • Morning: Travel to Kandy (about 2.5 hours by road).
    • Afternoon: Visit the Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya, known for its diverse plant species and bird life.
    • Evening: Explore Kandy’s cultural sites, such as the Temple of the Tooth Relic, and relax at your hotel.
  • Day 5 Kandy to Nuwara Eliya and Horton Plains
    • Morning: Travel to Nuwara Eliya (about 2 hours by road). Check into your hotel.
    • Afternoon: Take a trip to Horton Plains National Park (approximately 1-hour drive). Enjoy a walk and visit the famous World’s End viewpoint. Keep an eye out for sambar deer and the elusive leopard.
    • Evening: Return to Nuwara Eliya and enjoy the cool climate.
  • Day 6 Nuwara Eliya to Yala National Park
    • Morning: Travel to Yala National Park (approximately 4-5 hours by road). Check into a lodge or campsite near the park.
    • Afternoon: Go on an afternoon safari in Yala, which is famous for its high density of leopards, as well as elephants, sloth bears, and various bird species.
    • Evening: Enjoy dinner at your lodge and share stories of the day's sightings.
  • Day 7 Yala National Park and Departure
    • Morning: Take an early morning safari in Yala to maximize wildlife sightings.
    • Afternoon: Return to Colombo (about 4-5 hours by road) or travel to your next destination if your schedule allows.
    • Evening: If you have time, explore Colombo or relax before your departure.
