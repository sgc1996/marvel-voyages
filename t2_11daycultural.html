Eleven Day Cultural Tour

7 Days | 6 Nights

Tour Plans

  • Day 1 Anuradhapura
    • Morning:
    • - Arrival in Anuradhapura: Transfer from the airport or your previous location.
    • - Ancient City of Anuradhapura: Explore the significant archaeological sites including the sacred Bo Tree (Sri Maha Bodhi), Ruwanwelisaya Stupa, and the ancient royal palaces.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Local Lunch: Enjoy a traditional Sri Lankan meal in a local restaurant.
    • - Mihintale: Visit this important pilgrimage site where Buddhism is said to have been introduced to Sri Lanka.
    • Evening:
    • - Relax: Return to your accommodation and enjoy a restful evening.
  • Day 2 Sigiriya and Dambulla
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Sigiriya: Drive to Sigiriya, known for its impressive rock fortress.
    • - Sigiriya Rock Fortress: Climb the fortress and explore the ancient frescoes and panoramic views.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Sigiriya: Enjoy a meal at a local restaurant.
    • - Dambulla Cave Temple: Drive to Dambulla to visit the cave temples, featuring intricate Buddhist murals and statues.
    • Evening:
    • - Stay in Dambulla: Relax at your hotel or explore the local area.
  • Day 3 Polonnaruwa and Minneriya
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Polonnaruwa: A drive to this ancient city.
    • - Explore Polonnaruwa: Visit the well-preserved ruins, including the Gal Vihara rock temple, Royal Palace, and Parakrama Samudra lake.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Polonnaruwa: Enjoy a traditional meal.
    • - Minneriya National Park: Go on an afternoon safari to witness the large elephant herds and diverse wildlife.
    • Evening:
    • - Return to Dambulla or Sigiriya: Head back to your accommodation.
  • Day 4 Kandy
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Kandy: Drive to Kandy, the hill capital.
    • - Temple of the Tooth Relic: Visit this sacred Buddhist temple, housing a relic of Buddha’s tooth.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Kandy: Enjoy a meal at a local restaurant.
    • - Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya: Explore these beautiful gardens showcasing tropical plants and trees.
    • Evening:
    • - Cultural Dance Performance: Attend a traditional Kandyan dance show.
  • Day 5 Nuwara Eliya
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Nuwara Eliya: A scenic drive to Nuwara Eliya, known as “Little England.”
    • - Tea Plantation Tour: Visit a tea plantation and factory for a tour and tasting session.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Nuwara Eliya: Enjoy local cuisine with a view.
    • - Explore Nuwara Eliya: Visit Victoria Park and Gregory Lake. Enjoy a boat ride or a walk around the lake.
    • Evening:
    • - Stay in Nuwara Eliya: Relax at your hotel and enjoy the cool climate.
  • Day 6 Ella
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Ella: Drive to Ella, a picturesque town.
    • - Nine Arches Bridge: Visit this iconic bridge and enjoy the stunning surroundings.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Ella: Dine at a local restaurant.
    • - Ravana Falls: Explore these impressive waterfalls.
    • Evening:
    • - Return to Nuwara Eliya: Drive back for an overnight stay.
  • Day 7 Galle
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Galle: Drive to Galle on the southwest coast.
    • - Galle Fort: Explore this UNESCO World Heritage site, including its well-preserved colonial architecture and ramparts.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Galle: Enjoy seafood or local dishes in one of Galle’s restaurants.
    • - Explore Galle’s Old Town: Wander through narrow streets, visit local boutiques, and soak in the colonial charm.
    • Evening:
    • - Stay in Galle: Relax at your coastal accommodation.
  • Day 8 Colombo
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Colombo: Head to Colombo, the capital city.
    • - National Museum of Colombo: Visit the museum to learn about Sri Lanka’s history and cultural heritage.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Colombo: Enjoy local cuisine in the city.
    • - Pettah Bazaar: Explore this vibrant market area for a taste of local life and shopping.
    • Evening:
    • - Galle Face Green: Take a stroll along this popular oceanfront park.
  • Day 9 Negombo
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Negombo: A drive to this coastal town.
    • - Negombo Fish Market: Visit this bustling market to experience local seafood and market life.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Negombo: Enjoy a meal featuring local seafood.
    • - Dutch Fort: Explore the historical Dutch Fort, a remnant of colonial times.
    • Evening:
    • - Relax by the Beach: Enjoy the sunset and relax at Negombo Beach.
  • Day 10 Anuradhapura or Optional Exploration
    • Morning:
    • - Return to Anuradhapura: Drive back to Anuradhapura if desired.
    • - Optional Activities: Revisit favorite sites or explore any missed attractions.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Anuradhapura: Enjoy a traditional meal.
    • - Leisure Time: Relax or explore local markets.
    • Evening:
    • - Final Night in Anuradhapura: Enjoy a final evening in this historical city.
  • Day 11 Departure
    • Morning:
    • - Preparation for Departure: Depending on your flight time, you might have some last-minute shopping or relaxing.
    • - Travel to Katunayake Airport: Head to the airport for your departure.
