A Railway Tour Through The Northern

9 Days | 8 Nights

Tour Plans

  • Day 1 Arrival in Colombo
    • Morning: Arrive in Colombo and transfer to your hotel.
    • Afternoon: Explore Colombo's landmarks such as the Gangaramaya Temple, Independence Memorial Hall, and Galle Face Green.
    • Evening: Rest and prepare for the journey ahead.
  • Day 2 Colombo to Jaffna
    • Morning: Take the Kankesanthurai Express or Udarata Menike train from Colombo Fort to Jaffna. The journey offers scenic views and can take around 6-8 hours.
    • Afternoon: Arrive in Jaffna, check into your hotel, and rest.
    • Evening: Explore Jaffna’s vibrant streets and markets.
  • Day 3 Jaffna Exploration
    • Morning: Visit the Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil and the Jaffna Fort.
    • Afternoon: Take a trip to Dambakola Patuna and the Jaffna Library.
    • Evening: Relax at Casuarina Beach or Arugam Bay.
  • Day 4 Jaffna to Kankesanthurai and Return
    • Morning: Take a local train or a bus to Kankesanthurai (KKS) to explore the northernmost railway station in Sri Lanka.
    • Afternoon: Visit the nearby Kankesanthurai Harbour and enjoy a local meal.
    • Evening: Return to Jaffna by train or bus.
  • Day 5 Jaffna to Anuradhapura
    • Morning: Board a train from Jaffna to Anuradhapura. This journey can take about 2-3 hours.
    • Afternoon: Check into your hotel and start exploring Anuradhapura, including the Sri Maha Bodhi Tree, Ruwanwelisaya, and Jetavanarama.
    • Evening: Enjoy a peaceful evening exploring the town or relaxing at your hotel.
  • Day 6 Anuradhapura to Trincomalee
    • Morning: Travel by train from Anuradhapura to Trincomalee, a journey that typically takes around 3 hours.
    • Afternoon: Visit Fort Frederick and Koneswaram Temple. Enjoy the stunning views of the Trincomalee Harbor.
    • Evening: Relax on the beaches of Nilaveli or Uppuveli.
  • Day 7 Trincomalee to Batticaloa
    • Morning: Board a train from Trincomalee to Batticaloa. The journey offers a different perspective of the eastern coast.
    • Afternoon: Explore Batticaloa Fort and the Kallady Bridge.
    • Evening: Enjoy the serene beaches and perhaps a boat ride on the Batticaloa Lagoon.
  • Day 8 Batticaloa to Polonnaruwa
    • Morning: Travel by train from Batticaloa to Polonnaruwa. This journey usually involves a combination of train and bus travel.
    • Afternoon: Explore the ancient city of Polonnaruwa, including the Royal Palace, Gal Vihara, and Parakrama Samudra.
    • Evening: Return to your hotel and relax.
  • Day 9 Polonnaruwa to Colombo
    • Morning: Take a train or bus from Polonnaruwa back to Colombo. The journey might take around 6-8 hours.
    • Afternoon: Arrive in Colombo and enjoy any last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
    • Evening: Prepare for departure or spend the night in Colombo if needed.
