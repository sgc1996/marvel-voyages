Five Day Cultural Tour

5 Days | 4 Nights

Tour Plans

  • Day 1 Colombo and Negombo
    • Morning:
    • - Arrival at Katunayake Airport: Transfer directly to Negombo, a coastal town near the airport.
    • - Negombo Beach: Start with a relaxing stroll along the beach to unwind from your journey.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Negombo Fish Market: Visit the bustling market to experience local seafood and the vibrant atmosphere.
    • - Dutch Fort: Explore the historical Dutch Fort in Negombo, a remnant of colonial times.
    • Evening:
    • - Travel to Colombo: Head to Colombo, the capital city.
    • - Dinner in Colombo: Enjoy a meal at a local restaurant, trying traditional Sri Lankan dishes like rice and curry or seafood specialties.
  • Day 2 Colombo and Kandy
    • Morning:
    • - National Museum of Colombo: Start your day with a visit to this museum to get an overview of Sri Lanka’s history and culture.
    • - Galle Face Green: A leisurely walk along this large, open space by the ocean.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Travel to Kandy: Drive to Kandy, the hill capital, which takes about 3-4 hours.
    • - Temple of the Tooth Relic: Visit this sacred temple, which houses a relic of Buddha's tooth and is a key cultural site.
    • Evening:
    • - Kandy Lake: Enjoy a relaxing walk around the lake.
    • - Cultural Dance Performance: Attend a traditional Kandyan dance show in the evening.
  • Day 3 Sigiriya and Dambulla
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Sigiriya: Early departure for Sigiriya, known for its remarkable rock fortress.
    • - Sigiriya Rock Fortress: Climb the fortress and explore its ancient frescoes and the Lion’s Gate.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Dambulla Cave Temple: Drive to Dambulla to visit the impressive cave temples adorned with Buddhist murals and statues.
    • - Local Lunch: Enjoy a traditional meal in Dambulla.
    • Evening:
    • - Return to Kandy or Stay in Sigiriya: Depending on your preference, you can either return to Kandy for the night or stay in Sigiriya/Dambulla.
  • Day 4 Anuradhapura
    • Morning:
    • - Travel to Anuradhapura: Depart early for Anuradhapura, a significant ancient city.
    • - Explore Anuradhapura: Visit key sites such as the sacred Bo Tree, Ruwanwelisaya Stupa, and ancient royal palaces.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Mihintale: Visit this important pilgrimage site, believed to be where Buddhism was introduced to Sri Lanka.
    • - Lunch: Enjoy a traditional lunch in a local restaurant.
    • Evening:
    • - Return to Kandy or Colombo: Travel back to Kandy or head straight to Colombo, depending on your schedule.
  • Day 5 Colombo and Departure
    • Morning:
    • - Last-minute Shopping: Explore Colombo’s shopping areas such as Odel, Barefoot, or the Colombo City Centre for souvenirs and local crafts.
    • - Visit the Pettah Bazaar: A vibrant market area for a final glimpse of local life.
    • Afternoon:
    • - Lunch in Colombo: Enjoy a final Sri Lankan meal at a local restaurant.
    • - Departure Preparation: Head back to Katunayake Airport, ensuring you arrive with ample time before your flight.
